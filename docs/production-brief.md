## 🎵 MUSIC BRIEF (Suno)

**Overall vibe:** Piano-based, emotional, building. Not corporate. Not epic trailer. Human.

**Reference feel:**
- Olafur Arnalds — intimate piano + ambient
- Nils Frahm — organic, warm electronics
- Ólafur Arnalds' "Near Light" — simple, devastating

**Structure:**
- 0:00-0:30: Low drone, minimal piano notes, slightly unsettling ambient
- 0:30-1:15: Piano melody enters, warmer, more hopeful
- 1:15-2:00: Builds naturally — add strings or warm synth pad, rhythm emerges
- 2:00-2:20: Resolve on something simple. Maybe just piano and voice. Ending.

**Avoid:** 
- Heavy drops or EDM-style builds
- Corporate "inspiring" music
- Generic "cinematic" strings
- Anything that sounds like a tech commercial

**Suno prompt suggestion:**
"Intimate piano, ambient textures, emotional build, 120BPM, key of C major, warm analog feel, progressive, cinematic but personal, not epic, not corporate. Think Ólafur Arnalds meets Nils Frahm."
